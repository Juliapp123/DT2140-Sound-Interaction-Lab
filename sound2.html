    Interaction 1 - It is like a motorboat boat gas throttle, so when you point it up you are accelerating the boat and when it is horizontal you are not accelerating (the engine stops).
    Interaction 2 - When you do the hitting motion you hear the bell from a gong (like you are the one hitting it).
    Interaction 3 - When your phone drops (moves fast downwards), it may break and the thunder represents the phone breaking. When moving the phone sideways or slowly up and down, nothing happens.
